HISTORY

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts who believed in the power of collaboration and innovation. Over the years, it has grown into a global technology conference, attracting thousands of participants from startups, corporations, academia, and the public sector.

From its humble beginnings, TechCon has consistently showcased the trends and technologies shaping our future.

MISSION

TechCon exists to ignite innovation, foster meaningful connections, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Our mission is to create an inclusive space where ideas can thrive — a platform for sharing, learning, and driving technological progress with human-centered values at the core.
